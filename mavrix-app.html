📖 Mavrix Guide
How to get the most from Mavrix
Everything you need to know about each tool — when to use it, how it works, and what to try first.
⚡AI Social Agent
▼
What it does
Generates your weekly content calendar, trending topics, growth insights and opportunities — proactively, without being asked. It's your always-on Chief Marketing Officer.
When to use it
Every Monday to plan your week's content across all platforms.
Best for
Businesses that want consistent social media presence without the daily effort of figuring out what to post.

💡 Pro tip: The more detailed your Settings profile, the more personalised your calendar. Fill in your industry, audience, and value proposition for the best results.

💬AI Consultant
▼
What it does
Your always-on Chief Marketing Officer. Gives strategy advice, runs business health checks, analyses what competitors are doing in Singapore, defines your market positioning, identifies gaps you can exploit, and refines content — all in one conversation.
When to use it
When you have a marketing question, want a health check on your business, need to understand your competitive landscape, or want to define your unique position in the market. Use it anytime — it gets smarter with every conversation.
Best for
Business health checks · Competitor intelligence · Market positioning · Strategic recommendations · Campaign planning · Content refinement

💡 Pro tip: Start with "Do a full health check on my business" — it'll score your brand, audience, value prop, and channels out of 10 and tell you exactly what to fix first.

🚀Lead Growth Advisor
▼
What it does
Builds your personalised lead generation playbook and tells you exactly what to do when leads come in — step by step, specific to your business and the Singapore market.
When to use it
When you want more clients, don't know how to convert enquiries, or want a clear system for generating and following up on leads.
Best for
Getting new clients, converting leads, and building a repeatable sales pipeline.

💡 Pro tip: Generate your Lead Gen Playbook first (Section A), then use "Ask your Growth Advisor" for specific questions about your situation.

✍️Content Generator
▼
What it does
Writes social posts, email campaigns, and ad copy in your exact brand voice — fast, specific, ready to use.
When to use it
When you need specific content quickly — a post for a sale, a LinkedIn article, an Instagram caption for a product launch.
Best for
Quick content creation for specific occasions, campaigns, or platforms.

💡 Pro tip: Always add a brief — the more context you give, the more specific and usable the output. A vague brief gets a vague post.

📊Analytics
▼
What it does
Tracks your social media growth over time and shows your before/after Mavrix results — so you can prove the ROI of every hour you spend on content.
When to use it
Every week — input your follower numbers to track progress across platforms.
Best for
Seeing your ROI, identifying which platforms are growing fastest, and proving Mavrix is working.

💡 Pro tip: Enter your follower counts from before you joined Mavrix as your baseline. That way, your before/after comparison will actually show the difference Mavrix made.

📧Email Campaigns
▼
What it does
Writes and sends email campaigns to your contact list — from AI-generated copy to hitting send on a full list, all in one place.
When to use it
For promotions, newsletters, follow-ups, re-engagement campaigns, and event announcements.
Best for
Staying top of mind with your audience and converting warm leads into paying clients.

💡 Pro tip: Use the "Send to list" option with a CSV of your contacts for bulk campaigns. Upload a CSV with name and email columns to personalise each email automatically.
